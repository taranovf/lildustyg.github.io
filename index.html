Dustin Muriel
(December 23rd, 1999)

Dustin spent his formative years in Parkland, Florida before relocating to Dallas, Texas during his teenage years. He pursued his education at Frisco High School where he was an active member of the school's hockey team. His passion for the sport led him to participate in hockey games for various teams including HC Dallas, Frisco High, and McKinney North Stars, from the age of 15 until he turned 18. Overall, Dustin's experiences in both Florida and Texas helped shape his personal and athletic identity, leading him to develop a love for hockey that he carried with him throughout his youth.
